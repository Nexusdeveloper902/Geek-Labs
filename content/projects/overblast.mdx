Overblast is an arcade survival game inspired by the golden era of top-down shooters. I built it to learn more about custom rendering pipelines and particle systems in Unity.

## Mechanics
- Endless waves of procedurally generated enemies.
- Deep progression system with ship upgrades and skill trees.
- Beautiful neon vector graphics with custom bloom and post-processing.

## Development
The biggest challenge was optimizing the collision detection when there are hundreds of entities on screen. I ended up implementing a custom spatial hash grid which improved performance by 300%.
